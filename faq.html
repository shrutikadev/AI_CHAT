FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about your GenAI-Powered Financial Assistant

Is my financial data safe on this platform?

Yes, your data is protected using end-to-end encryption and industry-standard security protocols. We also support multi-factor authentication (MFA).

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the steps sent to your registered email address.

What can the AI assistant help me with?

The AI assistant can help you track expenses, create budgets, suggest investments, remind you about EMIs, and answer financial questions.

Can I set reminders for EMIs or bills?

Yes! Go to Profile > EMI Reminders to set due dates. You’ll receive notifications via email or the app.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! The platform is fully mobile-responsive and works smoothly on smartphones and tablets. A dedicated app is coming soon!

Is there a fee to use this assistant?

Basic features are free. For advanced analytics and investment tools, you can upgrade to our Premium Plan on the Pricing page.
